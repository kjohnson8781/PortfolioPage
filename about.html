About Me

Hey there! I'm Kara Johnson, a software engineer passionate about building tools that empower artists and developers.

I'm a recent engineering graduate from Texas A&M (Gig 'em!) and my journey to the tech industry was far from typical. If you had told me just a year ago that I would grow to love programming, I would have thought you were crazy. In fact, I hated my first computer science class in college.

However, life has a funny way of surprising us. While I initially pursued a different path, shortly after graduation, I seized an unexpected opportunity that revealed my true calling in software engineering. This pivotal decision reshaped my career path and led me to realize that I could leverage technology to directly help people - a core value I had been searching for in my earlier major and career decisions.

My transition from coding class skeptic to programming enthusiast has shaped me into a highly versatile engineer. I've honed my ability to rapidly adapt to evolving technologies and I'm continually learning new skills. I thrive in dynamic, high-pressure environments that allow me to collaborate with others to solve challenges. With my non-linear career path, I offer a unique perspective and bring a user-centered focus to all my projects. Through my work, I aim to enable artists and developers in transforming their creative visions into reality.

In my free time, I'm an avid sports enthusiast. Currently, I'm competing on a flag football team and training for a half marathon. I also enjoy spending time in nature, often embarking on camping and hiking adventures. When I'm not outdoors, you'll likely find me immersed in the virtual worlds of video games (The Last of Us is my favorite) or strumming my guitar, exploring the world of music.
