About Me

Hey there! I'm Kara Johnson, a software engineer and technical artist passionate about building tools that empower artists and developers.

I'm a recent engineering graduate from Texas A&M (Gig 'em!) and my journey to the tech industry was far from typical. In fact, I hated my first computer science class in college. However, shortly after graduation, I seized an opportunity that led me to realize I could leverage technology to directly help people - a core value I had been searching for in my earlier major and career decisions.

My transition to software development has shaped me into a highly versatile engineer. I've honed my ability to rapidly adapt to evolving technologies and I'm continually learning new skills. I thrive in dynamic, high-pressure environments that allow me to collaborate with others to solve challenges. With my non-linear background, I offer a unique perspective and bring a user-centered focus to all my projects. I love working at the intersection between technology and art, where I can apply my technical knowledge to tackle interesting problems and utilize my diverse experience to translate complex concepts and relate to others.

Besides working on my side projects, I love playing sports (currently learning hockey!) and going on camping and hiking adventures. In my remaining free time, you can find me immersed in video games (The Last of Us is my favorite), working out at the gym, or strumming my guitar.
